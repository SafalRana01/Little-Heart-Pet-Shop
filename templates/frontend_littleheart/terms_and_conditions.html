Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to Little Heart Pet Shop. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it. This Privacy Policy and Terms of Service explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, pet information, and booking details.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use our website (e.g., page visits, time spent).
  • Cookies: Our site may use cookies to enhance user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage grooming appointments.
  • To communicate with you regarding your bookings or inquiries.
  • To improve our services and website experience.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners for appointment processing or analytics purposes, but only when necessary and always securely.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications.

7. Terms of Service

a. Grooming Service Eligibility

We only accept pets that are healthy, non-aggressive, and suitable for grooming. We reserve the right to refuse services to pets showing signs of illness, severe matting.

b. Booking & Cancellation

All bookings must be made in advance. Please notify us at least 24 hours prior to cancel or reschedule your appointment.

c. Liability

While we take utmost care with your pets, grooming involves inherent risks. We are not liable for any pre-existing conditions or reactions related to grooming products or procedures.

d. Late Pickups

Pets must be picked up promptly after services. A late fee may be charged for extended stays beyond closing hours.

e. Senior & Special-Needs Pets

We may not provide grooming services for senior pets (18+ years) or those with medical complications. Please consult your vet before booking.

f. Aggressive Behavior

For the safety of both staff and animals, grooming services are not provided for pets exhibiting aggressive behavior. Should any signs of aggression arise during the grooming session, the service may be terminated immediately, and the pet owner will be promptly notified.
